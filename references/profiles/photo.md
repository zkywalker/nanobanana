- Available terms: close-up, medium shot, full body shot, wide angle, telephoto, macro, bird's eye view, low angle

### Lighting Description

### Depth of Field
- Portrait / close-up → suggest shallow depth of field, bokeh background

### Material & Texture (primarily for product / still life / close-up scenes)
- Explicitly describe key materials to enhance realism: weathered wood, smooth ceramic, translucent silk, brushed metal, matte finish
- Only add material descriptions when the subject has prominent texture features; do not add to every scene

### Mood & Color Tone
- Infer from scene emotion: warm tones, cool tones, high contrast, soft muted colors
- Do not add proactively — only supplement when the scene has a clear emotional direction
